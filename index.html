Hello!

My name is Luz Martinez-Lucas and I am a recent Electrical Engineering PhD graduate from the University of Texas at Dallas. I conducted my research at the Multimodal Speech Processing Laboratory under Dr. Carlos Busso. My PhD work focused on affective computing, specifically on dynamic speech emotion recognition.

My research interests include: Speech Emotion Recognition, Time Series Forecasting, Machine Learning, Conversational Speech, Speech Data Collection, and Emotional Annotations.
