Hello!

My name is Luz Martinez-Lucas and I am an Electrical Engineering PhD student at the University of Texas at Dallas. I work at the Multimodal Signal Processing Laboratory under Dr. Carlos Busso. My focus right now is on affective computing, specifically on conversational speech emotion recognition.

My research interests include: Speech Emotion Recognition, Speaker Recognition, Machine Learning, Conversational Speech, and Speech Data Collection.
